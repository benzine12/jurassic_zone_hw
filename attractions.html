Jurassic Park Attractions

T-Rex Encounter

Get up close with the king of dinosaurs! Our T-Rex Encounter allows visitors to experience the sheer power and majesty of the Tyrannosaurus Rex in a safe, controlled environment. Watch as our highly trained T-Rex demonstrates its incredible strength and agility, and learn fascinating facts about this apex predator of the Cretaceous period.

Book Now

Raptor Training Show

Watch our trainers work with velociraptors! This thrilling show demonstrates the intelligence and agility of these fascinating prehistoric predators. Learn about raptor behavior, hunting techniques, and the special bond between our trainers and these incredible creatures.

Book Now

Gyrosphere Valley

Roll through herds of gentle giants! Our Gyrosphere ride takes you on an unforgettable journey through the valley where our herbivorous dinosaurs roam freely. Get up close to Triceratops, Brachiosaurus, and more from the safety and comfort of our state-of-the-art gyrospheres.

Book Now

Cretaceous Cruise

Embark on a thrilling river adventure through dinosaur territories. Our Cretaceous Cruise takes you along ancient waterways where you'll encounter a variety of prehistoric creatures in their natural habitats. Keep your eyes peeled for surprises around every bend!

Book Now

Pteranodon Aviary

Witness prehistoric flying reptiles soar above you in our massive aviary. The Pteranodon Aviary offers a unique opportunity to observe these magnificent creatures up close. Walk through our specially designed enclosure and feel the rush of air as Pteranodons glide overhead.

Book Now

Mosasaurus Feeding Show

Watch our massive marine reptile leap from the lagoon during feeding time. The Mosasaurus Feeding Show is a spectacular display of prehistoric marine life. Witness the awesome power of this ancient sea predator as it demonstrates its hunting skills.

Book Now
