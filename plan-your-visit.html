Getting Here

Jurassic Park is located on Isla Nublar, off the coast of Costa Rica. Transportation to the island is provided by our private ferry service.

Park Rules

  • Stay in designated areas at all times
  • Do not feed the dinosaurs
  • Follow all safety instructions from staff
  • Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult

Accommodations

Stay at our world-class resort to fully immerse yourself in the Jurassic experience. From luxury suites to family-friendly rooms, we have options for every visitor.

Dining

Enjoy a variety of dining options throughout the park, from quick bites to gourmet restaurants featuring prehistoric-inspired cuisine.
