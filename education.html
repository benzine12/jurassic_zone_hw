Educational Programs

School Field Trips

Bring your class to Jurassic Park for an unforgettable educational experience. Our guided tours are tailored to different age groups and curriculum requirements.

Junior Paleontologist Program

Kids aged 7-12 can join our Junior Paleontologist Program, where they'll learn about dinosaurs through hands-on activities and exciting discoveries.

Dino Lectures

Attend our regular lecture series featuring world-renowned paleontologists and dinosaur experts. Perfect for adults and older students interested in the latest dinosaur research.

Interactive Exhibits

Our interactive exhibits allow visitors of all ages to experience what life was like during the age of dinosaurs, from touch screens to virtual reality experiences.
